Milktart

A South African dessert created by Dutch settlers in the Cape during the 17th century. Contains a sweet pastry crust, and has a bakers custard filling. Generally served at room temperature, and lightly dusted with cinnamon powder.

Pancakes

Also known as 'pannekoek', it is a South African version of a crepe, and a derivative of the Dutch Pancake. Traditionally served with cinnamon sugar and a small amount of lemon juice. It makes for a great base on which to serve savoury alternatives, since the batter contains little to no sugar.
