Milktart

A South African dessert created by Dutch settlers in the Cape during the 17th century. Contains a sweet pastry crust, and has a bakers custard filling. Generally served at room temperature, and lightly dusted with cinnamon powder.

Pancakes

Also known as 'pannekoek', it is a South African version of a crepe, and a derivative of the Dutch Pancake. Traditionally served with cinnamon sugar and a small amount of lemon juice. It makes for a great base on which to serve savoury alternatives, since the batter contains little to no sugar.

Snoek Braai

Snoek is a type of deep-sea fish that is found off the cost of Namibia, Northern Cape(ZA), and Western Cape(ZA). Typically served grilled with boiled sweet potato and has an Apricot and Lemon glaze. The fish is typically boney, although the meat itself is plentiful.

Rooibos Tea

Rooibos(Redbush) is a herb native to South Africa, more specifically, the Cederberg region. Originally harvested and consumed by the as a herbal remedy by the Khoi-San tribes as long as over 300 years ago, the modern version of Rooibos tea was created in 1907 by Russian immigrant Benjamin Ginsberg.
