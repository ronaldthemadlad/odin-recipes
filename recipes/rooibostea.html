Basic Rooibos Tea

This is a super simple, yet super effective tea recipe if you're looking to something with a wonderful taste and aroma, but will also help take the edge off at the end of a long day. You will be surprised to know that although it is called Rooibos Tea, it is actually a herbal tea since there are no tea leaves in this awesome aromatic blend! it is made from the Rooibos(Redbush) Plant, native to South Africa.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Coffee Mug
  • 2 Rooibos Teabags
  • 3 Teaspoons Sugar

    • Optional Ingredients:

  • 2 Teaspoons Honey
  • 2 Tablespoons Lemon Juice
  • 1 Thin Slice Fresh Ginger

Method:

  1. Place your teabags into you mug
  2. Pour boiling water into the mug and allow the tea to draw
  3. Remove the teabags
  4. Add your sugar and stir till sugar is dissolved
  5. Allow 2-3 minutes to slightly cool
  6. ENJOY!!

    7. Alternative Preparation:

  7. Follow steps 1-3
  8. Add the honey and lemon juice, stir until honey and lemon juice have dissolved
  9. Add thin slice of fresh ginger
  10. Allow 2-3 minutes to slightly cool
  11. ENJOY!!

Now that you have made you delicious, aromatic and soothing mug of Rooibos Tea, you should probably get warm and relax as you sit back and unwind from a long day, while drinking your new favorite, and natural, stress reliever! You could even chomp on a cookie or two while you're at it ;)
